## Project Overview

VORA is a **student-focused study app** designed to improve organization, focus, and mental well-being. In today's fast-paced education system, many students struggle to manage **classes, homework, exams**, and personal time. This often leads to stress, fatigue, and decreased productivity.  

VORA addresses these challenges by providing a **digital companion** that helps students plan their study sessions, track habits, and monitor mental health, all in one app.
